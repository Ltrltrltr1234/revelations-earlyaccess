The Catalyzer

The Catalyzer generates catalysts passively. The entropy value will scale with catalyst production rate, which is the rate of fluctuation of the dissonance value. Attune the assonance value to match the dissonance value to maintain the Catalyzer's integrity, and a highly accurate attunement will grant a bonus to catalyst production. If desynced for too long, entropy and production rate will decrease Integrity. If Integrity reaches zero, the Catalyzer is destroyed and it and its Upgrades will be reset. The Catalyzer does not reset upon singularity.

After disabling, the Catalyzer will remain disabled until a Singularity is created.

NOTE: Synchronisation can also be from one end of the slider to the other end. Think of it like a circle.
